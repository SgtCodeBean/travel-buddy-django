Backpacking Through Europe ✈️

⭐⭐⭐☆☆

📅 Day 1: Barcelona, Spain 🇪🇸

  • Morning: Arrive in Barcelona and check into a hostel like Hostel One Ramblas.
  • Midday: Visit La Sagrada Familia and Gothic Quarter.
  • Lunch: Try Paella at La Boqueria Market.
  • Afternoon: Relax at Barceloneta Beach.
  • Evening: Walk La Rambla and enjoy Sangria at a rooftop bar.

📅 Day 2: Nice, France 🇫🇷

  • Morning: Flight/train to Nice, check-in at Villa Saint Exupéry.
  • Midday: Walk the Promenade des Anglais, swim at the beach.
  • Lunch: Enjoy a Salade Niçoise.
  • Afternoon: Visit Castle Hill for stunning views.

📅 Day 3: Cinque Terre, Italy 🇮🇹

  • Morning: Train to La Spezia, stay in Riomaggiore.
  • Midday: Eat fresh seafood pasta by the sea.
  • Afternoon: Hike between Cinque Terre villages.

📅 Day 4: Rome, Italy 🇮🇹

  • Morning: Train to Rome, check-in at The Yellow Hostel.
  • Midday: Explore the Colosseum and Roman Forum.
  • Evening: Visit Trevi Fountain and enjoy Gelato.
